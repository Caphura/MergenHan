# Session Example

## Example 1 - Raw Notes To Draft

### User Request

```text
Elimde tam bir resume yok. Finali English olsun. Here are my notes:
- 5 years in operations and project support
- coordinated onboarding for new clients
- prepared weekly reporting packs for leadership
- cleaned up internal documentation and process notes
- worked closely with sales, support, and admin teams
- want to apply to Operations Coordinator roles

Here is the target job post summary:
- operations coordinator
- onboarding support
- cross-functional communication
- reporting and process improvement
```

### Good First Reply

```text
Bunu guclu bir English resume draftina cevirebilirim. Final drafta gecmeden once 3 seyi netlestireyim:

1. Bu notlarda ozel olarak sahip oldugun araclar var mi? Ornegin CRM, Excel, ticketing, scheduling tools gibi.
2. Somutlastirabilecegin bir etki ornegi var mi? Ornegin onboarding speed, reporting accuracy, process cleanup, stakeholder coordination gibi.
3. Bu rol junior / coordinator seviyesinde mi kalmali, yoksa daha advanced operations profile mi hedefliyorsun?
```

### Example Follow-up Answer

```text
- Main tools: Excel, HubSpot, Google Workspace
- I improved onboarding handoff consistency and made weekly reporting easier to prepare, but I do not have exact metrics
- I want coordinator-level roles, not manager-level
```

### Good Final Reply

```text
Candidate Summary

- Operations and project support professional with 5 years of experience in onboarding coordination, reporting support, and internal process organization.
- Strong cross-functional collaborator across sales, support, and administrative teams.
- Best positioned for coordinator-level operations roles with onboarding and reporting responsibilities.

Target Role Fit

- Strong alignment with onboarding support, reporting coordination, process improvement, and cross-functional communication.
- Relevant tools and workflow signals support an Operations Coordinator positioning.
- Missing exact metrics limit stronger quantified achievement claims.

Resume Draft

Professional Summary
Operations support professional with 5 years of experience coordinating onboarding workflows, preparing leadership reporting materials, improving internal documentation, and supporting cross-functional execution across sales, support, and administrative teams.

Core Skills
Onboarding Coordination | Cross-Functional Communication | Reporting Support | Process Improvement | Documentation Cleanup | Excel | HubSpot | Google Workspace

Experience Highlights
- Coordinated onboarding support activities for new clients, helping maintain consistent cross-team handoffs and smoother execution.
- Prepared recurring reporting materials for leadership, improving weekly visibility across operational updates and open actions.
- Improved internal documentation and process notes, making day-to-day operational workflows easier to follow and maintain.
- Supported collaboration across sales, support, and administrative teams to keep operational workstreams organized and moving.

Weak Spots / Missing Inputs

- No exact metrics yet for onboarding improvements or reporting efficiency gains.
- No formal job title history or date ranges were provided.
- Final draft can be strengthened further with 2-3 concrete achievement examples.

Optional Tailoring Notes

- If you share the full job post, I can tailor the summary and skills section more tightly.
- If you share date ranges and 2 quantified wins, I can upgrade the experience section further.
```

## Example 2 - Screenshot + Public Evidence + Local Artifact Reconstruction

### User Request

```text
Bu CV skill'iyle beni toparlamak istiyorum ama elimde temiz bir resume yok.
Sana LinkedIn deneyim ekran goruntusu, export edilmis bir PDF, GitHub repo'larim ve birkac public haber linki verecegim.
Finali English, one-page ve ATS-friendly olsun. Uydurma bilgi ekleme.
Ayrica export olunca bozulmus veya placeholder kalan yerler varsa soyle.
```

### Good First Reply

```text
Bunu temiz bir one-page English resume'e donusturebilirim. Once soruya donmeden sana ait inspectable kanitlari tarayacagim; ekran goruntusu, PDF, local repo ve public kaynaklardan cikarabildigim verified sinyalleri toplayip sadece gercekten kritik bosluk kalirsa kisa bir netlestirme turu acacagim.
```

### Good Working Notes

```text
Evidence tiers
- screenshot-or-document-verified: LinkedIn experience titles, date ranges, visible skills, education labels
- local-artifact-verified: repo names, engine/tooling stack, documented systems work
- public-source-verified: game showcase coverage, public company / event references
- user-asserted: ownership claims that are not independently visible yet
- inferred: positioning language built from the supported signals

Resume risks to catch
- placeholder address / phone left in export
- unsupported degree claims
- stacked-role progression flattened into one title
- generic corporate summary hiding game / technical strengths
```

### Good Final Reply

```text
Candidate Summary

- Game design and technical development profile with multi-year experience across game direction, systems-oriented design, narrative design, and hands-on engine work.
- Strongest positioning is at the intersection of game systems, playable production ownership, and technical workflow building.
- Best supported claims come from screenshot-verified experience history, local project artifacts, and public showcase coverage.

Resume Draft

Professional Summary
Game Director and Senior Game Designer with experience spanning game direction, game and narrative design, technical development, and gameplay-oriented production workflows across studio and independent environments. Built strongest evidence around systems thinking, cross-discipline execution, and game development workflows using Unity, Unreal Engine, C#, and C++.

Core Skills
Game Direction | Game Design | Narrative Design | Technical Game Design | Game Development | Unity | Unreal Engine | C# | C++ | Workflow Design | Cross-Functional Collaboration

Experience
- Anadox Teknoloji | Game Director | Mar 2026-Present
- Roof Games | Senior Game Designer, previously Game Designer | Dec 2023-Jan 2026
- Wizarel | Game Designer and Game Developer | Jun 2022-Sep 2023
- Independent | Freelance Unreal Engine Game Developer and Game Designer | Sep 2016-May 2022

Weak Spots / Missing Inputs

- Any claim about degree level should remain unverified until explicit confirmation.
- User-asserted ownership claims can be used carefully in final copy only if the user wants first-person ownership represented.
- Final PDF or Canva export should be checked for placeholder contact lines and merged layout artifacts.

Optional Tailoring Notes

- For gameplay designer roles, emphasize systems, combat, progression, tuning, and implementation partnership.
- For AI tooling or technical design roles, raise workflow design, technical documentation, and engine-facing project work.
```
